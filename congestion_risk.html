Why Risk Scoring?

Raw counts treat all locations equally. However, traffic impact is highly non-linear.

1 Scooter off-peak: PCS = 0.4
1 Bus double-parked peak: PCS = 9.0
A single bus blocks 22.5x more carriageway than a scooter. A non-linear Risk Score (0-100) captures this systemic hazard before absolute gridlock occurs.
